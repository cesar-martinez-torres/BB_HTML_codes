Your Course at a Glance

Course Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of digital design.
  • Apply combinational and sequential logic.
  • Code digital circuits using VHDL.
  • Use simulation and development tools.

Course Units

  • Unit 1: Codes and Numerical Systems
  • Unit 2: Binary Logic
  • Unit 3: Combinational Circuits
  • Unit 4: Sequential Circuits

Grading Scheme

  • First Partial Exam: 20%
  • Second Partial Exam: 20%
  • Activities and Homework (4 periods): 40%
  • Final Project: 20%
