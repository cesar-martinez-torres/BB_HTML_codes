Rules and Agreements for This Course

Activities and Lab Reports

Activities, homework, and lab reports must be submitted electronically on the course Blackboard site, respecting the due date and time indicated for each submission. Any homework/activity/lab report will not be accepted after the due date/time.

Student Responsibilities

  • The student is responsible for remaining at the university (or near campus) until the last day of final exams and for continually checking Blackboard, since all course information and communication will be made through the platform.
  • It is the student’s responsibility to know the General Student Regulations and their guidelines.
  • Attendance at exams and laboratory sessions is mandatory. Only health problems with a medical certificate issued by UDLAP will be considered.
  • If you disagree with your grade, please contact the instructor and request an appointment via Bookings.

AI Policies

The use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools is permitted under the following conditions:

Assignments (Homeworks)

  • It is recommended not to use AI, or to use it only in “Study and Learn” mode, as excessive reliance may negatively affect the learning process.

Laboratory Practices and Final Project

  • AI tools may be used as an aid.
  • You must explicitly document in the appendix the AI tool(s) used and the exact prompts applied.
  • It is strictly prohibited to generate all content using AI. AI may only be used as a supporting tool, not as the sole creator of the work.

Examinations

  • The use of AI is strictly prohibited during exams.
  • Any AI use will result in an automatic grade of 0.0.
  • Disciplinary actions may apply according to institutional regulations.

Submission Format

Normally, two files must be uploaded (each activity will indicate the details):

  • PDF document with the homework/activity/lab report.
  • ZIP or RAR folder with source code, videos, photographs, simulations, or any supplementary material.

If these requirements are not met, the homework/activity/lab report grade will be 0.0.

Academic Integrity

All submissions will be verified using anti-plagiarism software. If a similarity index greater than or equal to 35% is detected, the assignment will be graded with 0.0. Repeated offenses will be referred to the ethics committee.
